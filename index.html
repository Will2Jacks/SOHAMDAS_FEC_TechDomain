The Golden Boot
The FIFA World Cup Golden Boot, officially known as the Golden Boot Award, is presented to the top goal scorer of the FIFA World Cup tournament. The Audi Cup
The Audi Cup is a biennial pre-season football tournament hosted by the German club FC Bayern Munich. The tournament usually takes place at the Allianz Arena in Munich, Germany. The inaugural edition of the Audi Cup was held in 2009.



The PFA Young Player Award
The PFA Young Player of the Year award is an annual accolade presented by the Professional Footballers' Association (PFA) in England. It is awarded to the most outstanding young footballer aged 23 or under at the start of the English football season.
